# Privacy Policy for Exory File Manager

**Last Updated: March 8, 2024**

## Introduction

Exory File Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We do **NOT** collect any personal information. Exory File Manager is designed with privacy in mind and operates locally on your device.

### File Data
- The App accesses files and folders on your device only with your explicit permission
- All file operations are performed locally on your device
- No file data is ever uploaded to our servers
- File metadata (names, sizes, dates) remains on your device

### Usage Data
We do **NOT** track your usage patterns or collect analytics data by default. If you choose to enable optional analytics, we may collect:

- App version
- Device model and Android version
- Basic feature usage statistics (e.g., which features are used)
- Performance metrics

This data is **completely anonymous** and cannot be linked to you personally. You can disable analytics at any time in Settings.

### Crash Reports
If the app crashes, we may collect anonymous crash reports including:
- Stack trace
- Device state at time of crash
- App version
- Android version

These reports help us fix bugs and improve stability.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Improve and optimize the App
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Develop new features
- Respond to your requests and support inquiries

## Permissions

Exory File Manager requires certain permissions to function:

### Storage Permission
- **Android 10 and below**: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- **Android 11 and above**: MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- **Purpose**: To access, manage, and organize your files
- **Data accessed**: Only files you explicitly choose to interact with

### Internet Permission
- **Purpose**: Only for optional features like cloud sync and FTP server
- **Data transferred**: Only data you explicitly choose to sync or share
- **Note**: The app works completely offline without internet permission

### Biometric Permission
- **Purpose**: For app lock feature
- **Data accessed**: No biometric data is stored; Android handles authentication

### Camera Permission (optional)
- **Purpose**: To take photos directly in the app
- **Data accessed**: Only images you explicitly choose to capture

### Location Permission (optional)
- **Purpose**: To geotag photos
- **Data accessed**: Location data only when you explicitly choose to add geotags

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your files and data remain on your device
- No data is stored on our servers
- Encrypted files are secured with AES-256 encryption
- Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with salt

### Encryption
- Files can be encrypted with a password of your choice
- Encryption keys never leave your device
- We cannot recover encrypted files if you forget your password

### Third-Party Services

When you use optional features, your data may be handled by third-party services:

#### Cloud Storage
If you connect cloud accounts (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.):
- You control what files are synced
- Authentication is handled by the respective providers
- We don't store your cloud credentials
- Files are transferred directly between your device and the cloud service

#### FTP Server
When using the built-in FTP server:
- Files are transferred over your local network
- No data goes through our servers
- You can set a username and password for access
